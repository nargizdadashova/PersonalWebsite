Mugham

Mugham is a traditional genre of Azerbaijani music that combines classical poetry with complex musical improvisation. Ashiq Art

Ashiq Art is one of the oldest and most cherished oral traditions in Azerbaijan, blending poetry, storytelling, music, and dance into a unique form of folk performance. The practitioner, known as an ashiq, is a poet-singer who performs original and traditional verses while playing a stringed instrument called the saz.
